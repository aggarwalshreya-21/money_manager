Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Spendly ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our personal expense tracking service. By using Spendly, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information: your name, email address, and a securely hashed password.
  • Expense data: the categories, amounts, dates, and descriptions you record.
  • Technical data: basic logs such as IP address and browser type, used to keep the Service secure and reliable.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to operate, maintain, and improve Spendly. Specifically, we use it to authenticate your account, display your expenses, generate summaries and reports, and respond to your support requests.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or expense data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in our secure database. Passwords are stored only as one-way cryptographic hashes — we cannot read them. We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure.

That said, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Spendly uses only essential cookies — such as a session cookie to keep you signed in. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or cross-site trackers.

6. Your Rights

You may access, correct, export, or delete your account and data at any time from your profile page, or by contacting us. When you delete your account, your expense data is permanently removed from our active systems.

7. Children's Privacy

Spendly is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
